Bio

Welcome to UCscavenger, a space for the doers, the explorers, and the adventurers. If you're ready to start to get to know your UC campus, Embrace Discovery with UCscavenger.

Mission Statement

Our goal from day one has always been to show students our favorite spots on campus. We also wanted to foster our love of the outdoors throughout the entire student body of UCSC. Thus UCscavenger is born!
